RABAT BLACK MAMBAS

We bleed black's

Fonded in 2015, The Black Mambas is an American Football team from Rabat Morocco, with both a Male & Female section.

About Us

American football is a recent phenomenon in Morocco, where it appears in 2012 thanks to a group of friends who discover the practice on YouTube. A year later, the first team of the kingdom, the "Rabat Lions", is born. It will be followed soon after by the "Casablanca Stars" and "Tangier Dragons".

Attracted by contact sports, Sabrina, whose father is a former rugby player, began to take an interest in American football in 2013. The love at first sight was immediate, and the young woman was quick to live for and through football. Only problem: although her presence in the men's team is tolerated, she does not feel fully accepted.

At a summer camp in Italy, the coach denied her access to the field. Vexed, she sits on the bench and watchs all day. Back on the field the next morning, the coach, amazed by the determination of Sabrina, proposes to join them. From the snakes, she will have to swallow several during these first years. But nothing seems to be able to undermine her will, on the contrary. This is unfortunately not the case for the other girls who have tried to join the team, but will eventually become discouraged. Sabrina will remain for a long time the only player of the club.

Tired of these discriminations, Sabrina and two friends decided to form the Black Mambas in 2015, a team that welcomes both women and men. The beginnings are laborious. Due to lack of land, training sessions take place on the beach of Oudayas at the foot of the ramparts of the famous Kasbah classified as World Heritage by Unesco. They have no helmet or protection, injuries are common. It is not uncommon for some to play barefoot. But their obstinacy pays: the team is structured, the training becomes more intense and the phases of play more tactical.
